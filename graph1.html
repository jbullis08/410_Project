Distance vs Diameter by Albedo

This is a plot of distance versus diameter with the radius of the bubble a function of albedo. The purpose of this plot is to determine if any of the asteroids are going to be visible with the Pine Mountain telescopes. We are interested in asteroids with large diameter, small distance, and large albedo.
