Peak Day vs Magnitude by Albedo

This is a plot of peak day versus magnitude with the radius of the bubble a function of albedo. The purpose of this plot is to determine if any of the asteroids are going to be visible with the Pine Mountain telescopes. We are interested in asteroids with a peak day in between May and October, a low magnitude (bright) and a large albedo.
